Data Structures/Collection
String
- Collection of words, alphabets, sentences...etc...
- Represented in "" or '' or ''' ''' or """ """
List
- Collection of any type of data
- represented by []
Tuples
- similar to list but cannot be changed
- represented by ()
Dictionary
- collection of data in key:value pair
- represented by {key:value}
Sets
- collection of unique type of unordered data
- represented by {}
